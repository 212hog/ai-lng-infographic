LNG: The Foundational Power Solution

Natural gas provides the reliable, flexible, and scalable power essential for 24/7 data center operations.

Reliability

Provides stable, dispatchable baseload power, complementing intermittent renewables and ensuring 100% uptime for critical AI workloads.

Scalability

"Behind-the-meter" gas plants can be built directly on-site, bypassing grid delays and scaling with multi-gigawatt campus needs.

Efficiency

Modern combined-cycle plants achieve over 60% efficiency, with Combined Heat and Power (CHP) systems reaching up to 80%.
